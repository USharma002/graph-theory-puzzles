Minimum Spanning Trees (MST)

Problem Statement

Given a connected, undirected graph \( G = (V, E) \) with edge weights \( w(e) \), find a subset \( T \subseteq E \) connecting all vertices with minimum total weight.

$$ \text{minimize } \sum_{e \in T} w(e) $$

Kruskal's Algorithm

  1. Sort all edges by weight (smallest first).
  2. Add an edge only if it doesn't create a cycle.
  3. Stop when \( |V| - 1 \) edges are added.
